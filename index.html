What is DormDrop?

DormDrop is a monthly subscription box designed for busy college students. We deliver everything you need straight to your dorm room—no car trips, no stress, no empty snack drawer.

College students spend over $40 billion each year on dorm essentials. DormDrop saves time and money by offering reliable, affordable, customizable deliveries.
